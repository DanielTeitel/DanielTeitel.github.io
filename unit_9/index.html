Play Month

The Game

Play Month is one month of the year that takes play very seriously. By encouraging playfulness across Australia - at home, in the community, at school and at work - Play Month will facilitate important mental, physical and creative development for all Australians. Play Month also raises funds to build much needed playgrounds in developing countries, encourage healthy human development for the worlds youngest and most disadvantaged

How to Play

There are so many ways to play this month. Start by choosing a player below.

Individuals

Play as an individual - you can play alone, joining events, run play sessions for your friends and family

Groups

Join as a group, community organisation, sports team or loosely affiliated band of rebels.

Business

Bring play into your workplace. Find games and activities that build creativity, develop leadership skills, help solve problems and imagine new solutions.

Schools

Work together as a school to help giver other children around the world the chance to grow up healthy, happy and with a promising future.
